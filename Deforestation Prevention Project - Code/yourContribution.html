The Deforestation Prevention Foundation: Your Contribution With Us

The main way you can contribute to help prevent or reduce the effect of deforestation is by volunteerinng at our company based events in Canada. If you choose to volunteer at our events as a student in an Ontario secondary school, you will receive an official certificate with the number of hours of your contribution.

Companies are willing to provide us with the resources and equipment to help our communities recycle paper, plant trees to make a forest, and raise awarness to everyone you know and do not know!

Since this website's main purpose is to be a place for people and companies to gather, work toegther, and make a change in today's world, the next tab - Volunteering - will require to create an account for a better experience and for easier communication with companies in events and other volunteers in a forum board!
